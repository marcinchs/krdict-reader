Additional notes

I used *_base_* and *.exc files from Wordnet. These were combined with 2+2+3lem.txt file from 12dicst to create one file. Then all these words were looked up in National Institute of Korean Language's Korean-English Learners' Dictionary.

Inflections: 2+2+3lem.txt (from 12dicst) was combined with a base nouns list from Wordnet (which I updated with possessives singulars) and with Wordnet's *.exc lists.
